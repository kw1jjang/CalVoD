About

The Scalable Video-on-Demand System is developed by UC Berkeley Master of Engineering (M.Eng.) students Jiayuan Chen, Alagu Sanjana Haribhaskaran, and Ryan Kashi. It is a year-long capstone project advised by Kannan Ramchandran, Professor at UC Berkeley, and Kangwook Lee, doctoral student at BASiCS (Berkeley Audio-visual Signal processing and Communication Systems) Lab.
